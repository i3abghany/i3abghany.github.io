Mahmoud Abumandour

Mahmoud Abumandour is a PhD candidate at Simon Fraser University, supervised by Professor Alaa Alameldeen. His interests span software systems, computer architecture, and the boundary where hardware and AI meet.


His current work focuses on protecting deep learning applications on edge devices against fault injection attacks, with an emphasis on real-world reliability and attack-aware design.
